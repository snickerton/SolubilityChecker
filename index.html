Rule 1

All common salts of Group 1A (Akali Metals) and ammonium (NH4 +1) are soluble.

Rule 2

All common acetates (C2H3O2 -1) and nitrates (NO3 -1) are soluble.

Rule 3

All binary compounds of Group VII (halogens), other than Flourine, bonded with metals are soluble except for those compounds formed with silver, mercury, and lead.

Rule 4

All sulfates (SO4 -2) are soluble except those formed with barium, strontium, lead, calcium, silver, and mercury

Rule 5

Except for those in Rule 1, carbonates, hydroxides, oxides, sulfites, and phosphates, sulfides, chromates, are insoluble.
